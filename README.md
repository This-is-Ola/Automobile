# Automobile
This project analyses a dataset containing 22 different car makes to understand pricing distribution and fuel-type composition.
Project Overview

Aggregated total price by car make to identify which brands contribute the most value.

Created a visual comparison of car makes by total price, highlighting price concentration across brands.

a pie chart showing the distribution of cars by fuel type (Gas vs Diesel) to understand fuel preference patterns within the dataset.

Key Insights
Toyota, mercedes Benz ad BMW  account for a significantly higher share of the total market value respectively.

Gas-powered cars dominate the dataset compared to diesel, indicating stronger consumer preference or availability.

Visualisation makes it easier to compare brand value contribution and fuel-type split at a glance.
